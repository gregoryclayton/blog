Why So Lonely..?
A blog here, a blog there, articles on medium, more articles on substack, instagram facebook github blueskey, the channels for words have become free and multiplied. Alain Badou has said the role of philosophy at this point is to "fill in the cracks", and each online platrofrm serves as a nice little crack to fill. At this rate and structure though it seems inevitable that too any of thhese cracks will lead to collapse, filled or not.

In the words of the great Logan Roy, "TV's fucked, it needs to consolidate", So I've decided to crunch all my streams into this one handy little place instead.

Structure and Layout: most posts will include some rambling, three or so links to awesome stuff around at that time, a picture and maybe some new javascript interface er something I've been working on recently. longer posts will include a 'read more' button, while short posts (like this one) will be read completely on the front stream, but will still be included in the archive directory.

It's a throwback to oldschool blogging really, but looking at the layout in front of me on the screen, it kind of seems like a great idea for consolidation of the major internet platofrms of teh day as well; taking this old-school blogging layout and applying it to the consildating video hosting, social media, online shopping, whatever the user wishes. For now, it remains lonely.
